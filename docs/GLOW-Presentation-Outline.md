# Presentation: GLOW -- Magical Large Print Workflows
**Duration:** 45 Min Presentation | 15 Min Q&A
**Theme:** Accessibility isn't a chore; it's a superpower.

---

## 1. The Hook: The "Blink" Test (5 Mins)
*   **Visual Aid:** Show a standard 12pt Times New Roman agenda vs. a GLOW-fixed 18pt Arial agenda.
*   **Talking Point:** "In the time it took you to blink, a user with low vision decided whether they could participate in your meeting or not. Accessibility is the difference between an invite and a barrier."
*   **The Problem:** Most Office documents are 'born inaccessible'—small fonts, jagged edges, and invisible structure.

---

## 2. What is GLOW? (5 Mins)
*   **Concept:** Guided Layout & Output Workflow.
*   **Talking Point:** "GLOW is a community project by BITS (Blind Information Technology Solutions), an affiliate of the American Council of the Blind. It's the first toolkit built *by* the low-vision community *for* the world."
*   **The Three Pillars:**
    1.  **Audit:** Know exactly what's wrong.
    2.  **Fix:** Magic wand for Word and Markdown.
    3.  **Convert:** Move between formats without losing accessibility.

---

## 3. Demonstration: The 60-Second Fix (10 Mins)
*   **Action:** Screen share the live site at `glow.bits-acb.org`.
*   **The Flow:**
    1.  Upload a "messy" Word document (italics everywhere, 10pt font, bold text instead of headings).
    2.  Run the **Fix** tool.
    3.  **The Reveal:** Open the fixed document to show Arial 18pt, proper heading styles, and underlined emphasis.
*   **Talking Point:** "GLOW didn't just change the font. It fixed the *soul* of the document by adding semantic structure that screen readers can finally understand."

---

## 4. Deep Dive: Power Features (10 Mins)
*   **Faux Heading Detection:** Explain how GLOW uses AI/Heuristics to find "fake" headings (bold text) and turn them into real H1/H2 tags.
*   **BITS Whisperer:** Mention audio transcription—turning recorded board meetings into accessible meeting minutes instantly.
*   **Rules Reference:** Show the new inclusive labels ("Include Shown", "Exclude Shown").
*   **Talking Point:** "You are the conductor; GLOW is the orchestra. You have total control over every rule, from alt-text requirements to binding margins."

---

## 5. Pro Tips: Getting the Best out of GLOW (10 Mins)
*   **The "Audit-Fix-Audit" Sandwich:** Audit first to triage, Fix to automate, then Audit again to see the 5% that needs a human touch (like descriptive alt-text).
*   **Markdown is King:** Suggest extracting text to Markdown for heavy editing before converting to a final PDF or HTML.
*   **The Template Shortcut:** Don't fix later—use the **Template** tool to create an "accessible-by-default" Word file today.

---

## 6. The Closing: The "Magical" Impact (5 Mins)
*   **Talking Point:** "When we Use GLOW, we aren't just checking boxes for compliance. We are ensuring that the 250 million people worldwide with vision loss have a seat at the table. That is the real magic."
*   **Call to Action:** "Go to GLOW today. Fix one document. Change one person's day."
