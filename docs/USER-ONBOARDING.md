# GLOW User Onboarding Guide

Welcome to GLOW! This guide explains how to log in, what to expect, and who to contact for help.

---

## How to Log In
1. Go to the GLOW login page.
2. Click “Sign in with Google”.
3. Log in with your Google account (Gmail or Workspace).
4. You’ll be redirected back to GLOW and signed in.

---

## Who Can Log In?
- Any user with a Google account (Gmail, Workspace, etc.).
- Special features (facilitator/admin) require extra permissions (see below).

---

## If You Need Facilitator or Admin Access
- Contact your GLOW administrator.
- They will assign you the correct role in the system.
- Log out and back in to see new features.

---

## Privacy and Security
- GLOW uses secure, standards-based login (OIDC via Keycloak).
- Only your email and basic profile info are used for login.
- No passwords are stored in GLOW.

---

## Troubleshooting
- If you can’t log in, make sure you’re using a Google account.
- If you need special access, contact your admin.
- For technical issues, contact: [admin email here]

---

Enjoy your GLOW experience!
