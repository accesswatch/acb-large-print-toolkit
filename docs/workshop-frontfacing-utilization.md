# Utilization Guide

Use this guide to deploy Workshop Mode for conferences, onboarding, institutional training, and community practice.

## Pre-Event Readiness
- Confirm Workshop Mode feature flags.
- Validate workshop route and activity routes.
- Validate keyboard-only flow and screen-reader smoke pass.
- Prepare fallback worksheets and short URLs.
- Confirm facilitator support contacts and escalation path.

## During Delivery
- Start with participant problem statements.
- Reframe specialist role from fix-only to capability building.
- Apply responsible-AI boundary consistently.
- Require explicit human-review checkpoints in all outputs.
- Use gallery and peer feedback to increase trust and transfer learning.

## Post-Event Follow Through
- Export and distribute workshop artifacts.
- Package top workflows into prompts/checklists.
- Run 30-day follow-up for adoption and confidence tracking.
- Identify high-value workflows for promotion into GLOW skills.

## Governance and Quality
- Keep clear boundaries for AI assistance and human accountability.
- Preserve participant privacy with optional anonymity.
- Avoid sensitive data in shared artifacts unless explicitly approved.
- Keep a repeatable facilitator runbook and quality checklist.

## Success Metrics
- completion rate by activity
- capstone completion and quality
- percentage of artifacts with human-review safeguards
- 30-day workflow reuse rate
- participant confidence delta

## Recommended Institutional Pathway
1. Pilot with one team.
2. Gather artifact quality and adoption evidence.
3. Standardize best workflows as templates.
4. Expand to cross-functional partner cohorts.
5. Report outcomes and iterate quarterly.
