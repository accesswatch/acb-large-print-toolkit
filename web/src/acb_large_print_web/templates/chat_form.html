What can I ask?

Guided Question Cards

  • Compliance card: "Run an accessibility summary and give me the top 5 issues."
  • Critical-only card: "List critical findings only and explain the impact."
  • Structure card: "Check heading hierarchy and show skipped levels."
  • Faux heading card: "Find faux headings and suggest proper heading levels."
  • Link quality card: "Find ambiguous link text and recommend replacements."
  • Emphasis card: "Check italic and bold emphasis patterns for ACB compliance."
  • Remediation card: "Prioritize fixes by impact and effort."
  • Rule explainer card: "Explain rule ACB-LINK-TEXT in plain language and how to fix it."

Agent Categories Available

  • Compliance Agent: score, critical findings, auto-fixability
  • Structure Agent: heading hierarchy, faux headings, list structure
  • Content Agent: section search, link text quality, emphasis checks
  • Remediation Agent: rule explanations, fix suggestions, prioritization

Example Questions

  • Search questions: "Where is the term 'accessibility' mentioned?" or "Find all prices in the document"
  • Structural questions: "List all headings" or "Show me table 2"
  • Summary questions: "Summarize the Methods section" or "What's the key conclusion?"
  • Statistics: "How many pages is this?" or "What's the reading time?"
  • Comparison: "How does the Introduction differ from the Conclusion?"

Accessibility-focused tools the AI can call:

  • Run compliance summary and severity analysis
  • Extract and display tables
  • Find specific sections by heading
  • List and validate heading hierarchy
  • Find faux headings and alignment risks
  • Evaluate link text quality and emphasis patterns
  • Get document statistics (word count, reading time, etc.)
  • Prioritize fixes and explain specific ACB rules
  • Summarize sections
