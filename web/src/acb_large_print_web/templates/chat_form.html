What can I ask?
  • Search questions: "Where is the term 'accessibility' mentioned?" or "Find all prices in the document"
  • Structural questions: "List all headings" or "Show me table 2"
  • Summary questions: "Summarize the Methods section" or "What's the key conclusion?"
  • Statistics: "How many pages is this?" or "What's the reading time?"
  • Comparison: "How does the Introduction differ from the Conclusion?"

Tools the AI can use:

  • Search for text patterns
  • Extract and display tables
  • Find specific sections by heading

  • Get document statistics (word count, reading time, etc.)
