Welcome to the GLOW Accessibility Toolkit

GLOW (Guided Layout and Output Workflow) is a free, open-source accessibility tool sponsored by Blind Information Technology Solutions (BITS), a special interest affiliate of the American Council of the Blind. It helps authors and organizations produce documents that meet the ACB Large Print Guidelines and WCAG 2.2 Level AA accessibility standards.

GLOW can audit, fix, convert, and transcribe documents and audio files entirely on this server. No commercial account, subscription, or login is required.

How GLOW Handles Your Files

Documents (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, PDF, Markdown, ePub)

  • Your file is uploaded directly to this server over an encrypted HTTPS connection.
  • It is processed in a private temporary folder identified only by a random token. No filename, content, or metadata is logged.
  • The file and all outputs are automatically deleted within 1 hour of upload, and immediately after you download your results.
  • Your document is never shared with, forwarded to, or stored by any third party.
{% if ai_whisperer_enabled %}

Audio Files (BITS Whisperer)

  • Audio files you upload for transcription are sent to the Whisper API for high-quality speech recognition and then immediately deleted from the GLOW server.
  • Transcripts are processed locally and never stored beyond your session (deleted within 1 hour).
  • The audio file is deleted from GLOW immediately after the transcript is received from the API.

AI-Assisted Features

  • Optional AI features route requests through GLOW's configured AI provider (OpenRouter). Only the content required to complete a request is transmitted.
  • Transmitted content is not sold, shared, or used for advertising or independent product training by GLOW.
  • AI-assisted options are always optional, clearly labelled, and can be skipped entirely.

Cookies and Storage

GLOW uses one consent cookie and optional device-local storage:

  • Consent cookie (glow_consent_v1): stores the fact that you have read and agreed to these terms. It expires after 365 days. No personal data is stored in it -- it contains only the value 1.
  • Saved preferences (optional): if you choose to remember your defaults, those choices are stored in your browser's local storage on your device only. This can include workflow defaults, custom rule sets, and Rules Reference filters. Nothing from that storage is sent to the server.
