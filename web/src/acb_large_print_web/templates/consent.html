What GLOW Does

GLOW (Guided Layout and Output Workflow) is a free, open-source accessibility tool sponsored by Blind Information Technology Solutions (BITS), a special interest affiliate of the American Council of the Blind. It helps authors and organizations produce documents that meet the ACB Large Print Guidelines and WCAG 2.2 Level AA accessibility standards.

GLOW can audit, fix, convert, and transcribe documents and audio files entirely on this server. No commercial account, subscription, or login is required.

How GLOW Handles Your Files

Documents (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, PDF, Markdown, ePub)

  • Your file is uploaded directly to this server over an encrypted HTTPS connection.
  • It is processed in a private temporary folder identified only by a random token. No filename, content, or metadata is logged.
  • The file and all outputs are automatically deleted within 1 hour of upload, and immediately after you download your results.
  • Your document is never shared with, forwarded to, or stored by any third party.

Audio Files (BITS Whisperer)

  • Audio files you upload for transcription are processed using a local copy of the Whisper medium model running on this server.
  • Your audio never leaves this server. It is not sent to OpenAI, any cloud speech API, or any other external service.
  • The audio file is deleted immediately after the transcript is returned to you.

AI-Assisted Features (Heading Detection)

  • The optional AI heading-detection feature uses a locally hosted language model (Ollama with Llama 3) running on this server.
  • Document text sent to AI heading detection stays on this server. It is not forwarded to OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, or any external API.
  • AI features are always optional and clearly labelled.

Cookies and Storage

GLOW uses two types of cookies:

  • Consent cookie (glow_consent_v1): stores the fact that you have read and agreed to these terms. It expires after 365 days. No personal data is stored in it -- it contains only the value 1.
  • Session preferences (optional): if you use the Settings tab to save your defaults, those choices are stored in your browser's local storage on your device only. Nothing is sent to the server.

GLOW does not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, analytics services, or any third-party cookie. There is no Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or similar tool on this site.

No Accounts. No Tracking. No Ads.

  • No user account or login is required or collected.
  • No IP addresses are stored or associated with your uploads.
  • No usage statistics, behavioral analytics, or session recordings are collected.
  • GLOW has no advertising. It will never be monetized through your data.

GLOW is a public service tool. Your documents are your business.
